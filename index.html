Critical Findings

Critical — PulseCredit (Article 9) No formal risk management system in place. No documented methodology for identifying AI-specific risks. Required before August 2, 2026.
Critical — PulseCredit (Article 10) Training data bias assessment has not been conducted. Credit bureau data may reflect historical lending discrimination against protected groups.
High — PulseCredit (Article 14) Human oversight is ad hoc. No structured HITL workflow, no override logging, no operator training programme.
High — PulseConnect (Article 26) Third-party AI provider has not supplied conformity documentation. Deployer obligations cannot be fulfilled without provider cooperation.
Info — PulseGuard Fraud detection AI is exempt from high-risk classification under Recital 58. Only Article 50 transparency disclosure required.

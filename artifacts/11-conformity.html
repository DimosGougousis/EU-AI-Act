EU Declaration of Conformity
Annex V Format — Regulation (EU) 2024/1689 (EU AI Act)

1. AI System Name and Version:
PulseCredit v2.1 — AI Credit Scoring and Loan Origination System

2. Provider Details:
FinPulse NL B.V.
Herengracht 182, 1016 BR Amsterdam, Netherlands
KVK: 87654321 | LEI: 549300FINPLS2026NL01

3. AI System Description:
PulseCredit v2.1 is an AI system for the automated assessment of creditworthiness of natural persons and generation of credit recommendations for consumer loan applications ranging from €500 to €25,000. The system operates as a high-risk AI system pursuant to Annex III, Point 5(b) of Regulation (EU) 2024/1689.

4. Intended Purpose and Deployment Context:
Used by FinPulse NL B.V. in the context of its Consumer Credit Provider authorisation under the Wet op het financieel toezicht (Wft), the Netherlands. Deployed exclusively within the FinPulse mobile application for Dutch consumers aged 18+.

5. Declaration:
FinPulse NL B.V., as provider of the above-named AI system, hereby declares that PulseCredit v2.1 has been developed and is maintained in conformity with the requirements of Regulation (EU) 2024/1689 of the European Parliament and of the Council on Artificial Intelligence (EU AI Act), specifically:

  • Article 9 — Risk Management System
  • Article 10 — Data and Data Governance
  • Article 11 and Annex IV — Technical Documentation
  • Article 12 — Record-Keeping and Logging
  • Article 13 — Transparency and Provision of Information to Deployers
  • Article 14 — Human Oversight
  • Article 15 — Accuracy, Robustness, and Cybersecurity
  • Article 27 — Fundamental Rights Impact Assessment
